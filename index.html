Starting Early

Why start the 11+ journey now?

The intense demand for grammar school places across Trafford means your child needs every advantage they can get. Waiting until Year 5 often leads to a stressful, high-pressure cramming phase that can damage a child's love for learning.

By starting early, we have the time to gently build and embed solid skills in Maths, English Comprehension, and Non-Verbal techniques. We grow a richer vocabulary and refine core arithmetic without the pressure cooker environment.

More time means more practice, deeper confidence, and significantly lower stress for both you and your child.
