About Your Tutor

Dedicated to Your Child's Success

When you choose South Manchester Tutoring, you aren't getting a random university student or an overwhelmed agency worker. You are getting our full dedication. We are a team of experienced private tutors with over 12 years of specialized expertise in 11+ coaching and GCSE preparation.

Having worked extensively with leading tuition companies in both Manchester and London, we've seen firsthand what works—and what doesn't. We brought that knowledge back to Sale (M33) to create a more personalized, effective, and lower-stress approach for local families aiming for the Trafford grammar schools.

Our Teaching Philosophy

True learning doesn't happen in a state of panic. Our philosophy is rooted in building foundational understanding and supreme confidence. Whether we are preparing for a high-stakes 11+ exam or catching up on GCSE Maths, we believe in breaking down complex topics into digestible, manageable pieces.

The SMT Commitment

We believe that premium, effective education shouldn't be out of reach. That's why we offer highly competitive, affordable rates. To ensure your child never feels lost during the week, our program includes extra free online lessons, plus all materials, tests, and homework—no hidden surprises.

We're not just here for the lessons. We provide round-the-clock support and regular feedback to assure parents and guide students every step of the way. Let's turn "I can't" into "I just did."
